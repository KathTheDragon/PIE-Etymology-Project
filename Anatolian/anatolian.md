# Hittite

## Note on Transcription
Several liberties have been taken with the spelling of Hittite forms in bound transcription. Firstly, the letters _إ،_ and _ل¸«_ have been replaced with _s_ and _h_. Secondly, the distinction between _p_, _t_, and _k_, on the one hand, and _b_, _d_, and _g_, on the other, has been collapsed in favour of the voiceless stops, as I know of no functional contrast that employs these spellings.

## Word classes
Verbs have been annotated with the form of the 3sg present corresponding to their inflection class: [zi] (_mi_-conjugation), [i] (_hi_-conjugation), [ari] (dentalless middle), and [ttari] (dental middle). Nouns are annotated with their gender: [c.] (common gender) and [n.] (neuter gender).
